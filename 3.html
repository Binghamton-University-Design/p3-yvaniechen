Pork Rib Stew With Potatoes

Materials

Potatoes, Pork Rib,Ginger, Spring Onion, Soy Sauce, Salt Sugar

Process

Slice the potatoes into pieces and soak them with salt water

Slice the pork rib and soak them to remove blood

Drain all soaked materials

Stir fry the ginger slice with oil and then add the ribs in

Pour some soy sauce and stir several time

Pour hot water that can cover the materials in and add potatoes to stew with cover on

Open the cover and saucing with somme salt, sugar, and spring onion before turning off the stove

Story

This is the dish I love the most and it is my mother's special dish to cook. Although it seems very easy to cook, I have cooked for many times during my period in college, tried to get the taste in my memory and I always failed. Till I returned home in summer in 2019, I finally got it. It is my mother's care that cannot be replaced. The touch of care is the key for this dish when cooking.
