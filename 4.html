Chicken Wing Stew With Winter Melon

Materials

Winter Melon, Chicken Wings, Spring Onion, Ginger, Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce, Salt, Sugar

Process

Slice the winter melon and chicken wings into pieces

Stir fry the ginger slice with oil and then add the wings in

Pour some soy sauce and oyster sauce then stir several time

Pour hot water that barely cover the materials (less than half) in and add potatoes to stew with cover on

After well-mixed, put the soup in without bones and boiled for some time

Open the cover and saucing with same salt, sugar, and spring onion before turning off the stove
